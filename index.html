Privacy Policy for Tap Snap

Last updated: March 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is handled by the mobile game Tap Snap, developed by Fussion Labs.

Information We Collect

Tap Snap does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

However, the app uses certain Apple services that may collect data independently:

  • Game Center (for high scores and achievements)
    Apple may collect Game Center-related information in accordance with its own privacy policies.
  • Local Storage
    The app saves your high score, settings (background, sound, etc.), and game preferences locally on your device. This data is never transmitted off your device.

Third-Party Data Access

We do not share your data with any third parties.
We do not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking libraries.

Children's Privacy

Tap Snap is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.
